Monarch

Automated Quran recitation videos

Monarch creates short Quran recitation videos and publishes them to the creator's own connected social accounts, on a schedule.

What it does

  • Selects Quran verses and assembles a short vertical video with recitation audio and on-screen text.
  • Adds calm background footage and renders a finished clip.
  • Publishes the video to the account owner's own TikTok and YouTube — only accounts they explicitly authorize.

How it uses TikTok

  • The account owner authorizes Monarch through TikTok's official login (scope user.info.basic).
  • Monarch posts the rendered video to that same account via the Content Posting API (scope video.publish).
  • No content or data from any other TikTok user is accessed.
