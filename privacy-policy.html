Privacy Policy

Monarch · Last updated: 15 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Monarch ("the Service", "we", "us") handles information when you connect an account and use the Service to publish short Quran recitation videos to platforms you own, including TikTok and YouTube.

1. Information We Access

DataWhy
OAuth access & refresh tokensTo authenticate with the platforms you connect (e.g. TikTok) and publish content on your behalf. Tokens are refreshed automatically so the Service can run on a schedule.
Basic profile info (open ID, display name, avatar)To confirm which account is connected and to query posting eligibility before uploading (TikTok scope user.info.basic).
Video content created by the ServiceTo upload the generated recitation video to your authorized account (TikTok scope video.publish).

2. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to operate the Service you have configured: generating videos and publishing them to the account(s) you explicitly authorize. We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

3. What We Do Not Do

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own purposes. We do not access content or data belonging to anyone other than the connecting account owner. We do not collect data from other TikTok users.

4. Third-Party Services

To function, the Service exchanges data with: TikTok and YouTube (to publish videos), Google Gemini (to select verses and generate titles), Pexels (background footage), and Quran data providers (recitation audio and verse text). Each is governed by its own privacy policy.

5. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored within your own self-hosted automation environment and are used only to make authorized API requests. Generated video files are temporary and deleted after publishing. We take reasonable measures to protect stored credentials, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

Tokens are retained only for as long as needed to operate the Service and are replaced on each refresh. You can delete stored tokens at any time by removing them from your environment.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You can revoke the Service's access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok: Settings and privacy → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions. Revoking access immediately stops the Service from posting. You may similarly disconnect any other connected platform.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children and is intended for use only by the account owner operating it. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
